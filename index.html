    Mini Game Design Document

    Bullet Hell Game

    1. Concept:
    Bullet Hell Game is a fast-paced arcade shooter where the player controls a ship to survive against waves of enemies. The goal is to destroy as many enemies as possible while avoiding enemy projectiles. The game tests the player's reflexes and shooting accuracy in a challenging and intense gameplay experience.

    2. Gameplay:
    - Player Controls: The player controls a ship using arrow keys or touch controls on mobile devices.
    - Shooting Mechanics: Pressing the space bar or tapping the screen shoots a bullet from the ship towards enemies.
    - Enemy Mechanics: Enemies spawn at the top of the screen and descend downwards. The player must destroy them by colliding with their ship's bullets.
    - Score System: The player's score increases for each enemy destroyed.

    3. Features:
    - Player Ship: Control a ship represented by a blue rectangle, capable of moving left and right.
    - Bullets: Shoot red bullets represented by small red rectangles to eliminate enemies.
    - Enemies: Enemies appear as green and purple rectangles, descending from the top of the screen.
    - Visuals and Audio: Utilize simple geometric shapes to represent the ship, bullets, and enemies. Clear color distinctions enhance visual clarity.

    4. Conclusion:
    Bullet Hell Game is an engaging and challenging arcade shooter that provides an intense gameplay experience. With its simple controls and fast-paced mechanics, players will test their reflexes and shooting skills as they strive to achieve the highest score. The game's minimalistic visuals and lack of audio effects keep the focus on the gameplay itself. Whether played in short bursts or longer sessions, Bullet Hell Game offers an exciting and addictive gameplay experience for players seeking an action-packed challenge.

  • Infinite Runner

    "Infinite Runner" is an HTML5 game where the player controls a character that runs endlessly, avoiding obstacles to achieve a high score.
